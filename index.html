  2. Life & Friends
    • Nannan, roommate, painting teacher, fond of cuisine and all delicate things, single, her room is decorated and settled really neat and clean with lines of dolls on the table and earrings, necklaces in the porcelain boxes. She makes me feel like I am not a real Girllll~ There are several pieces of oil paintings in her room, which are all about climbers. I was astonished and really excited about her room the first time I saw it because she makes me feel she is living a real life. She says: I don’t want to compromise to daily life and I want to make everyday look like as the way I enjoy. I really appreciate that attitude.
    • MK Wang
    She is a colleague in my team and came back from America with master degree majoring in Geography information system. She came to MS just one year and half. The first time I saw her is when she returned from USA for business trips and at the team meeting, she introduced to us what she had done there and what she had seen. I was attracted by her personality. Once I asked her Do you like your work or are you satisfied? She replied, Yes, of course. I like the free atmosphere here and even with heavy burden, you will be rewarded and people here don’t care how you achieve the goal, if you did it, ok, it doesn’t matter how you managed doing that.

    I picked and bought a set of fancy and practical porcelain tableware on the internet and I was really happy and enjoyed myself the moment I tore open the package and touched them. I was surprised to find myself so delightful to appreciate the handicraft works and I saw a sentence like this, many people lose the small funs on the way finding huge pleasure. I think this is the small fun for me.
    • Swimming
    Well, I picked up swimming again and I really enjoyed it. I learned how to breaststroke and swimming for me is a total release. Hang on, baby!
